Deskmat Design Project

A personal design project creating custom deskmats tailored for gamers and students. The deskmats are designed to serve dual purposes — as a functional desk mat for organizing workspace and as a high-quality mouse pad for precise gaming control.

Key Highlights
  • Designed for both gamers and students with ergonomic sizing in mind
  • Created multiple visual themes to cater to different audience preferences
  • Optimized artwork for large-format printing (900×400mm standard deskmat size)
  • Tested color accuracy across different screen calibrations and print outputs
  • Explored material-specific design considerations for rubber-base cloth-top deskmats
